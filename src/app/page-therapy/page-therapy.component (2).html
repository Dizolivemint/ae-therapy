What is psychotherapy? The Greek root words of psychotherapy translate into the healing of the mind, soul, breath, spirit. Therapy is an intentional time that is set aside to tend to the parts of ourselves that we often overlook, ignore or may have felt we needed to hide for various reasons. The therapeutic relationship provides a safe and confidential (legal exceptions to confidentiality will be discussed in our first meeting) space for clients to deepen self-understanding, improve patterns of communication, strengthen relationships, relieve symptoms and heal trauma, among many other unique goals. Therapy often offers some immediate relief and feelings of greater well-being, and therapy can also take time and be challenging. My intention is that we can discuss openly any challenges or unmet needs throughout the therapeutic process.
